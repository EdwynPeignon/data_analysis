Conclusion¶

I'll say that streamroot have a good percentage of repartition of his p2p. 140716 users are giving bandwidth to 345037 users. We can see that it is actually a good result. My warnings are:

  • Strange result of the movie stream 3. There are a lot of users watching it, using multiple browsers but it represents just 1% of the p2p distribution.
  • The two browsers Vectrice and Swamp which are so low in the p2p score
  • The isp Datch Telecom which are desperately low for the use of p2p.
